Club Merchandise

Show your support for The Dorset Bikers Club with official club gear and essentials.

Our merchandise range is designed for real riders — practical on the bike, smart enough for meets, and always flying the Dorset Bikers colours with pride. Stock, designs and sizes will grow over time, so keep an eye on this page and our social media for new drops and pre-order announcements.

What We Offer

  • Shirts & Hoodies – Club-branded casual wear for ride-outs, meets and everyday use.
  • Hats & Gloves – Caps, beanies and light gloves to keep you comfortable on and off the bike.
  • Bags & Essentials – Practical kit for carrying your gear, plus everyday items no rider should be without.
  • Tools – Handy basic tools and small bits to help with quick adjustments and checks.
  • Mugs & Key Rings – Perfect for the garage, office or as gifts for fellow Dorset Bikers.

Full product details, prices and ordering information will be added soon. In the meantime, keep an eye on our Links page and social media channels for the latest announcements.
