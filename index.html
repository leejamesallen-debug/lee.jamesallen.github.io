Dorset Bikers Club

Friendly rides • Great people • Proper Dorset roads

A welcoming motorcycle community for riders of all experience levels across Dorset. From coastal runs and charity ride-outs to café meets and evening socials, there’s always a ride – and a brew – on the go.

More Than Just a Ride

Whether you’re on your first 125 or your tenth bike, you’ll find friendly faces, safe group riding and plenty of laughs along the way.

Regular Ride-Outs

Planned group rides around Dorset, the coast and beyond – sensible pace, clear routes and plenty of fuel and coffee stops.

All Riders Welcome

New riders, returning bikers and seasoned riders are all welcome. No attitude, no pressure – just a good, safe ride.

Events & Charity Runs
